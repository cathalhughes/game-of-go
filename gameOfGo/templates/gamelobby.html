Welcome To Our GO - Game Lobby

This is the main homepage for our game of GO. From here you can choose to start a game as Black, join a game as White and view the Online Leaderboard.

If you choose to start a game you will be asked to wait for another player and you will start as black. If you choose to join a game you will be added to somebody else's game and will start second. The leaderboard will show the Top 10 users of the game.
